### Part 1
## What happens when you press the Power Button?

```text
Power Button
      ↓
Power Supply
      ↓
Motherboard
      ↓
CPU Reset
      ↓
Firmware (BIOS/UEFI)
      ↓
Hardware Check
      ↓
Bootloader
      ↓
Operating System
      ↓
Desktop Appears
```

```text
      ↓
Application

Screen Update
```
